What We Do

Our team develops hyper-casual games for mobile platforms that can captivate players from the first click. We focus on simplicity of mechanics, exciting visuals, and analytics to maximize user retention. Our games are simple yet engaging mechanics that allow each player to quickly immerse themselves in the game.

About Us

We’re an independent studio specializing in hyper-casual games. Our focus is creating engaging games that are easy to understand, visually appealing, and fun to play.

For us, hyper-casual is more than just a genre — it’s about delivering maximum emotions in minimal time. We aim to make games that are instantly addictive, provide quick satisfaction, and leave a lasting impact.

With several completed projects behind us and big goals ahead, we’re constantly refining our process. We’re open to working with publishers who value creativity, reliability, and quality. Ready to take it to the next level.
